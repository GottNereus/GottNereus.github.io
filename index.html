GLÖGG

Glögg is a Scandinavian mulled wine made from red wine, cognac and spices, such as cinnamon, cardamom, ginger and cloves. In addition, raisins are served, which can be added to the warm glögg. Traditionally, glögg is drunk during Advent. In the Christmastime, glögg is offered everywhere in Sweden and also in Finland at any time of the day.

Ingredients
Cinnamon

You need:
2-3 sticks of cinnamon

Orange

You need:
One bio orange

Cloves

You need:
About ten pieces of cloves

Cane Sugar

You need:
150g of cane sugar

Ginger

You need:
2cm of ginger

Raisins

You need:
50g of raisins

Wine and Cognac

You need:
One bottle of red wine and 1dl cognac

Preparation

Cut the ginger and the orange in small slices like in the picture above. Put the wine,cognace and all the other ingredients including the cloves in a pan. If you like you can refine the recipe with wodka and almods. Cover the pan and bring the glögg to boil. Keep it covered and let stand for approx. two hours on the switched off plate.

Result
Skål
After two hours or when you want to drink, fill your mug with glögg and add a slice of orange if you wish.
Now there`s just enjoing left
